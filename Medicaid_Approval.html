Once the Asset Protection Plan has been implemented and the application has been filed, "Approval" is anticipated around day 45, give or take. During this period, the applicant's legal representative will be kept aprised of the filing status, any additional requests for documentation, as well as, the amount of monthly income remaining after the Asset Protection Plan. This amount, the "Patient Responsibility" will be paid to the nursing facility each month going forward.


Once the "Approval" is attained, Medicaid will cover the cost of care above the "Patient Responsibility" going back retroacitvely to the first of the month for the month the applicant became eligible. 


The Key to attaining the "Approval" status lies in the process. Our process has been proven effective for over 20 years and 6,000 applications.
